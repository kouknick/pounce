A divider is nothing more than a simple line for separation.

Can be vertical:

```typescript jsx
<Flex>
  <Box as="span">Hello</Box>
  <Divider orientation="vertical" />
  <Box as="span">World</Box>
</Flex>
```

Or horizontal:

```typescript jsx
<Box>
  <Box>Hello</Box>
  <Divider orientation="horizontal" color="red" />
  <Box>World</Box>
</Box>
```
